Founder & Growth Strategist

Abhinash Singh

CEO & Co-Founder

Abhinash Singh is an entrepreneur, business strategist, and wellness-focused leader with experience across finance, sales, digital growth, and brand development. As the CEO and Co-Founder of Reka Creative Labs, he specializes in helping businesses scale through modern marketing, website development, and growth-focused digital strategies.

With a strong background in sales leadership and financial analysis, Abhinash has worked with leading organizations including Paytm, BYJU'S, Testbook, and 1West Finance. Alongside his entrepreneurial ventures, he is also the Managing Director of Shyan Health, an Ayurvedic wellness brand focused on sustainable health transformation.

He holds certifications in finance and business analysis from Yale University, Harvard Business School Online, and the National Institute of Securities Markets. His academic background in Computer Science further strengthens his analytical and technology-driven approach to building impactful, customer-centric brands.
